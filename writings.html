Writings

Writing is telepathy. ~Stephen King

I am a big fan of the writing process. Writing helps me clear my head and think coherently. Many a times it enables me to generate new ideas. In my opinion, writing is a tool for better conversation, first with yourself, then with others. Perhaps, there is something magical about the human ability of symbolic representation of ideas.

Mostly, I try to write about the things I read and at times about some crude thoughts I have. Everything published here are my personal opinions and do not represent the institutions I am affiliated to. I would love to connect if you found anything useful or interesting. Shoot me an email: purnendu@colorado.edu.
